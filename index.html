Rocks come in many diferent sizes,shapes and colours,but they are all made up of pieces called minerals.What the rock is like depends on the Minerals it contains. There are three main types:igneous, sedimentery and metamorphic rocks.

Igneous Rocks

Igneous Rocks are usallyhard. They form when molten magma cools.

Metamorphic Rocks

Metamorphic Rocks form when exsisting rocks are heated and squeeze.

Sedimentary Rocks

Sedimentary Rocks form when layers of sand or mud are dried and crushed together.Graduly,they form another layer of rock.
